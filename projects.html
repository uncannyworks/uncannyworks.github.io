Dungeon Slammers

Coming 2015!

Dungeon Slammers - An exciting billiards-like combat, RPG styled smartphone game.

Dungeon Slammers will be a free to play, simple but fun little RPG-style dungeon crawler for iOS (and maybe Android!). Inspired by old school games like Wizardry, Legend of Zelda, and new school games like Monster Strike out of Japan.

Core gameplay involves unlocking new areas of the world map, equipping your hero with a powerful weapon and unique armor, entering various dungeons full of treasure, combating handfuls of enemies with a party of four, and finally defeating the boss for a chance at some sweet loot. By slingshotting your characters around the map, you can collide with enemies for damage, or your party members for heals, damage boosts, and more!

You begin on Noob Island, entering increasingly harder dungeons, fighting your way through monsters in a billiards style of gameplay, all while attempting to increase your power, unlock new and exciting skills, and meet/make new friends to build your party. Combat takes place across different color themed sections of a world named Slammeria, where you will encounter angry Green druids, stoic White angels, disease ridden Black zombies, flame covered Red fire elementals, or aloof Blue wizards and their brethren.

NinMuS

Coming December 2014!

NinMuS - Work Management made simple.

任務: (NinMu) Japanese. A task, mission, or duty.
S: English. Append to a noun to indicate more than one.
任務S: A webservice to help people get stuff done!

Built upon basic K.I.S.S. principals NinMuS is a tool to track tasks.

Simple enough for a mother to track chores for her kids, but powerful enough for organizations to track work throughout a company.
